# 🗑️ ARMS - One-Page Summary

## Automated Refuse Management System
**Making Waste Management Work for Everyone**

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Nigeria's 774 Local Government Areas spend **₦180+ billion annually** on waste management—yet most cities remain dirty, residents are frustrated, and governments have zero visibility into operations. **ARMS digitizes the entire waste management value chain.**

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**ARMS** is a complete SaaS platform connecting residents, waste operators, and local government administrators:

### For Residents:
✅ Collection schedules & notifications  
✅ Online bill payment (Paystack)  
✅ Recycling rewards & digital wallet  
✅ Interactive location maps (GPS)  
✅ Complaint submission & tracking  

### For Administrators:
✅ Real-time fleet management (15 vehicles, 6 drivers)  
✅ Automated billing & payment tracking  
✅ Route optimization & scheduling  
✅ Performance analytics & reporting  
✅ Complaint resolution system  

### For Operators:
✅ Digital route cards  
✅ GPS-verified service completion  
✅ Real-time issue reporting  
✅ Performance tracking
