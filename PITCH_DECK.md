# 🗑️ ARMS: Automated Refuse Management System
## Transforming Waste Management Through Technology

---

## 📊 Executive Summary

**ARMS** is a comprehensive digital platform that modernizes waste management operations for local governments and municipal authorities. We connect residents, waste operators, and administrators in one intelligent system that eliminates inefficiencies, improves service delivery, and promotes environmental sustainability.

**Live Demo**: https://arms-roan.vercel.app  
**Status**: Production-ready, fully functional  
**Target Market**: Local Government Areas (LGAs), Municipal Authorities, Private Waste Management Companies

---

## 🎯 The Problem

### Current Waste Management Challenges:

1. **Zero Visibility**
   - Residents don't know when collection happens
   - No way to report missed pickups or issues
   - Manual complaint tracking is inefficient

2. **Operational Inefficiency**
   - Paper-based routing and scheduling
   - No real-time fleet tracking
   - Manual billing and payment collection
   - High operational costs

3. **Low Resident Engagement**
   - No recycling incentives
   - Poor communication channels
   - Payment friction and disputes
   - Low compliance rates

4. **Revenue Leakage**
   - Manual billing prone to errors
   - Delayed payments
   - No digital payment tracking
   - Revenue collection inefficiency

**Result**: Poor service delivery, resident dissatisfaction, and mounting waste management costs that burden local governments.
