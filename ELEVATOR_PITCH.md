## Objection Handling

### "Government contracts are hard to win."
"That's why we offer a free 3-month pilot with performance guarantees. No upfront cost, prove value first. Plus, we're partnering with state-level authorities who can mandate adoption."

### "Why hasn't anyone done this before?"
"They have—in Europe and US. But international solutions don't fit African infrastructure. We're built for Nigerian conditions: works offline, USSD-ready, integrated with local payment systems."

### "What about competition?"
"We're the only complete solution. Others offer point solutions (just tracking, or just billing). We do everything. Plus, we're live and working—not just a prototype."

### "Can you scale?"
"Absolutely. Our cloud-native architecture is built to handle 100+ LGAs from day one. Each new customer is largely self-service after implementation. Marginal cost per LGA is minimal."

### "What about payment collection?"
"We integrate with Paystack, bank transfers, and are adding USSD and cash agent networks. Multiple fallback options. In pilot, we achieved 70% digital payment adoption—unprecedented in Nigerian LGAs."
